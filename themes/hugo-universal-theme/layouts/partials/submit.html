Do you Want to Collaborate in the Observatory?

The online Observatory of Talent Management Practices in Asian Universities is a place where academics, non-academics, professionals, and any stakeholder can share their knowledge and experiences with the university community. Therefore, the observatory is open to anyone who wishes to collaborate and contribut. There are three types of possible collaborations:

  • Adding a new News item on Talent Management in Universities.

  • Adding a new Talent Management Practice in Universities.

  • Updating or improving some of the current Talent Management Practices in Universities.

  • Providing recommendations for the future.
